Your wedding day moves fast. While your photographer captures the formal moments, a photobooth lets your guests create their own — candid, authentic, and completely their own. From your 90-year-old grandmother to your youngest flower girl, everyone loves it.

And with our unmanned setup, you're not managing another vendor all night. It just runs. Beautifully.
